About the Founder

Santhoshini – Visionary Artist. Global Learner. Heart-led Creator.

This didn't start in a fancy studio. It started in front of a bedroom mirror, with a kajal pencil and a wild curiosity.

Some people discover their passion. Others are born with it. For Santhoshini, beauty was never just a hobby — it was an instinct.

From a young age, she was fascinated by the way a little color, care, and confidence could transform not just how someone looked — but how they felt. While most saw makeup as a tool, she saw it as a medium of self-expression, emotion, and empowerment.

Her journey began with self-learning — teaching herself through tutorials, practice sessions, and experimenting on friends and family. But her dream was bigger: to turn her talent into a true craft.

She soon found herself in the heart of London, studying at the prestigious Academy of Freelance Makeup (AOFM) — a decision that shaped her into a world-class artist. Her time at AOFM gave her more than technical skills; it gave her exposure, confidence, and a deeper purpose.

Over the years, Santhoshini has worked at international fashion weeks, editorial shoots, and high-end bridal projects, learning from global industry leaders and refining her signature style — one that blends technique with intuition, glamour with authenticity.
